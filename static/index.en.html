ABOUT US

AAJ Legal Advice and Consultancy S.A.S., is an incorporated company, that provides advice to non-legal and legal persons, both within the private and public sector and that includes provision of multiple services, such as contractual operations, resource application, fulfils investmens and acts as an agent or representative of Colombian and foreign corporations.

MISSION

AAJ Legal Advice and Consultancy S.A.S., is a company that provides legal advice and litigation consultancy to its clients; Colombian or foreign, non-legal or legal, whilst maintaining ethical responsibility and professional performance, through the use of all the skills and qualities that the fellows can effectively use to achieve all their goals.

VISION

To be an organization of experts in recognized law in the field of professional litigation at the national and international level, outstanding for the quality of its advice, for the vocation of service to clients, the competence of representation and the effective fulfillment of the commitments acquired , All within the limits imposed by the political constitution and the laws.

Legal Advice and Consultancy, in the development of its corporate purpose, collects personal data from its employees, customers, shareholders and suppliers that have the status of natural and / or legal person.


Area of Application

The Policy will be applicable to databases in physical or electronic format that are under the administration of Legal Advice and Consultancy and it will act as Responsible, provided that:

  • The treatment of the data of individuals is carried out in Colombian territory.
  • The person in charge or the person in charge of the treatment does not reside in Colombia, however, according to international norms or treaties, Colombian law is applicable.

Scope

The provisions and mechanisms contained in this policy, cover all those personal data, recorded in databases contained in digital or physical media.

These databases are grouped into the following groups:

  • Clients: administered in digital and physical form.
  • Providers: administered in digital and physical form.
  • Employees: administered digitally.
  • Shareholders: it is managed in physical form and rests its contents in the Book of Registration of Shareholders.

The types of data that are treated in the referenced bases are the following:

  • Public: public data are all those that are contained in public documents, duly executed judicial decisions not subject to reservation and those relating to the civil status of the persons.
  • Semi-private: semi-private data are all those that are not intimate, reserved or public in nature and which are of particular interest for a specific sector.

Interpretation of Principles

Law 1581 of 2012 will be interpreted in the sense to adequate protection of constitutional rights such as habeas data, the right to good name and reputation, the right to privacy and the right to information. The rights of the holders shall be interpreted altogether and in a level of balance with the right to information provided for in Article 20 of the Constitution, as with all related rights.


Treatment and Purposes

Legal Advice and Consultancy, may directly or jointly with third parties in charge, perform the processing of personal data supplied by the holders. The data will be kept in the databases duly classified by Legal Advice and Consultancy.

The purposes of the treatment are as follows:

  1. Develop the contractual relationship established with the Data Holder.
  2. Evaluate candidates to be linked as officers of the Company.
  3. To develop the labour relations that exist with its employees.
  4. Know and monitor the financial suitability and commercial behaviour of its suppliers.
  5. Conduct surveys in order to know their tastes and preferences and thus be able to develop services that respond more directly to their needs; and improve the service rendered both in opportunity and quality.
